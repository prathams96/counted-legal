About these pages

These public pages are the official support and legal home for Counted.

Counted is a local-first app with no account system, no cloud backend, and no third-party analytics. The documents here are written to match the app's launch behavior and App Store review expectations.

If Counted later adds premium purchases, cloud features, analytics, or crash reporting, these policies should be updated before those changes ship.
